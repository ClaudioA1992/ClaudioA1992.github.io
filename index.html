Quien soy

Hola, soy Claudio, músico hace 15 años. Empecé a tocar la guitarra por la intensidad que me transmitió el heavy metal, y esa pasión me llevó a introducirme en otros estilos y a hacer de esto mi estilo de vida. Aquí pretendo mantener un pequeño portafolio de lo que hago, y de los servicios que ofrezco. Sientente libre de comentarme lo que opines.

Who am i

Hi, i'm Claudio, a musician since 15 years. I started playing guitar because of the intensity that heavy metal transmitted, and that pasion took me to introduce myself into another styles and make this my lifestyle. My parents knew each other being musicians, and i always listened to them to sing and play their instruments since i was a kid. I can say i'm privileged because of that; it's something i carry in my veins. Here i pretend to maintain a little portfolio of what i do, and on the services i offer. Feel free to comment me what's your opinion on this.
